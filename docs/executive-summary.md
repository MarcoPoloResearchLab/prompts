## The Opportunity

AI assistants are now core productivity tools for hundreds of millions of knowledge workers. But there's a hidden bottleneck: **the prompt**. Users spend significant time crafting, refining, and re-inventing prompts—often with inconsistent results. Meanwhile, prompt creators have no scalable way to monetize their expertise.

| Pain Point | Who Feels It |
|------------|--------------|
| Blank-page problem | Every new AI user |
| Inconsistent outputs | Teams lacking prompt standards |
| No monetization path | Expert prompt engineers |
| Fragmented discovery | Everyone searching Reddit, Twitter, Discord |

**Prompt Bubbles is building the marketplace that connects prompt creators with prompt consumers.**

## Beyond Text: The Full Spectrum of Generative AI

AI is no longer just chatbots. A new generation of tools generates images, video, music, voice, code, and 3D assets—each with its own prompting language and best practices. Most users struggle to unlock these capabilities because **prompting varies dramatically across modalities**.

Prompt Bubbles is building a **unified marketplace that spans the entire generative AI landscape**:

| Modality | Tools | Prompt Complexity |
|----------|-------|-------------------|
| **Text & Chat** | ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Llama | Structured instructions, personas, chain-of-thought |
| **Image Generation** | Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, Flux | Style keywords, composition, negative prompts, aspect ratios |
| **Video Generation** | Sora, Runway, Pika, Kling | Scene descriptions, camera movements, temporal consistency |
| **Audio & Music** | Suno, Udio, ElevenLabs | Genre, mood, instrumentation, voice characteristics |
| **Code & Development** | Copilot, Cursor, Replit AI | Context framing, constraints, output formatting |
| **3D & Spatial** | Meshy, Luma, emerging tools | Geometry hints, texture descriptions, rigging instructions |

**Each modality attracts distinct audiences—designers, musicians, developers, marketers, filmmakers—yet all share the same core need: prompts that actually work.**

By supporting every AI capability under one roof, Prompt Bubbles becomes:
- The **single destination** for AI-powered creators across disciplines
- A **cross-pollination hub** where text prompt engineers discover image techniques (and vice versa)
- **Future-proof** as new modalities emerge (AI agents, robotics, scientific modeling)
